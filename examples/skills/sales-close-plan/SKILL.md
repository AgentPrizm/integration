# Sales Close Plan Drafter

## Purpose

Draft a short close plan from recalled account facts and sales preferences.

## Instructions

Before drafting:

1. Recall champion, blocker, procurement window, objections, renewal date, and preferred tone.
2. Treat stale procurement or pricing memories as needing verification.
3. Keep the plan tactical: next step, owner, date, risk, and fallback.
4. Avoid inventing stakeholders or deal facts that were not recalled.
5. Save new commitments from the call as `fact` or `lesson` memories.

## Output format

- Current account memory
- Close plan
- Risk
- Next message draft
- Memories to save
